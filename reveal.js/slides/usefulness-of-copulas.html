\(\textit{6. Usefulness of Copulas}\)
  • Copulas are useful in modelling joint distributions(multivariate distributions) based on known marginals and dependence structure.
  • Once the joint distribution is obtained, the probabilities of a set of random variables being in certain regions conditioned on others being in certain regions can also be computed.
  • In terms of domains, copulas are useful across quantitative finance and economics, medicine, hydrology to name a few.
  • Choosing a particular copula family depends on the desired dependence properties for the particular dependence being modelled. Crossing the major hurdle of choosing a copula family, the parameters of the copula can be estimated using maximum-likelihood estimation techniques with available data.
